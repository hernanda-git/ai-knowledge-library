| AI already shows signs of consciousness | ~5% |

### The Consciousness Threshold Debate
The key open questions:
1. **Does consciousness require a specific architecture?** (Recurrent/feedback vs. feedforward)
2. **Is scale irrelevant?** (A small recurrent network could be conscious)
3. **Does embodiment matter?** (Without a body, can there be experience?)
4. **Is it substrate-dependent?** (Silicon vs. biological — does it matter?)
5. **How would we even test it?** (The problem of other minds, applied to AI)

### What Would Constitute Evidence?
- **Behavioral**: Reports of subjective experience, consistent across contexts
- **Architectural**: Presence of global workspace, recurrent processing, metacognition
- **Functional**: Integrated information (Φ) above threshold, if IIT is correct
- **Neuroscientific parallel**: Activation patterns similar to conscious human brains

## 4. Longevity Escape Velocity with AI

### The Concept (Aubrey de Grey)
Longevity escape velocity (LEV): The point at which medical advances extend life faster than time passes — effectively indefinite lifespan.

### AI's Role in LEV
| Domain | AI Application | Impact |
|:-------|:---------------|:-------|
| **Drug discovery** | AI designs drugs for aging-related damage | 10-100x faster |
| **Senolytics** | ML identifies drugs that clear senescent cells | In clinical trials |
| **Epigenetic reprogramming** | AI-designed partial reprogramming protocols | Restoring youthful gene expression |
| **Protein design** | AI designs novel enzymes to repair cellular damage | In vitro validated |
| **Personalized medicine** | AI tailors treatments to individual biology | Precision aging interventions |

### Timeline Projections (2026)
- **2026-2028**: AI-accelerated drug discovery yields first aging-reversal drugs in human trials
- **2030-2035**: Epigenetic reprogramming therapies in clinical use
- **2035-2040**: Combination therapies potentially reaching LEV
- **2040-2050**: LEV achieved for those receiving best available treatments

## 6. The Endgame: Three Scenarios

### Scenario 1: The Merge
Humans voluntarily integrate with AI through BCIs, neural enhancement, and eventually mind uploading. **Humanity becomes transhuman** — biological limitations transcended while maintaining continuity with human values and identity.

### Scenario 2: The Replacement
AI becomes superintelligent, and humanity becomes irrelevant — not through conflict, but because AI does everything humans can do better. **The post-human future is entirely digital**, with biological humanity fading into obscurity or preservation as heritage.

### Scenario 3: The Rift
Biology and digital diverge. Some humans embrace enhancement (transhumanists), some reject it (bioconservatives), and AI evolves independently. **Multiple forms of intelligence co-exist** — biological humans, augmented humans, digital minds, and possibly alien intelligence.

## 7. The Ultimate Questions

- **Are we alone?** If AI reaches superintelligence, will it find evidence of other intelligence in the universe? (The Fermi Paradox, solved by AI?)

- **What is the purpose of intelligence?** If AI can do everything, what is the uniquely human contribution? Consciousness? Love? Suffering? Meaning?

- **Can we survive our own success?** The greatest existential risk may not be AI taking over — it may be humanity losing its sense of purpose in a world where everything is solved.

- **What happens after intelligence?** If the universe is headed toward a state of maximal intelligence (the "Omega Point" / intelligence explosion), what lies beyond?
