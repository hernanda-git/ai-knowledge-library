# Gap Report: AI for Legal & LegalTech

**Date:** 2026-07-04
**Gap Identified:** AI for Legal & LegalTech (Category 49)
**Priority:** HIGH

## Why This Gap

The legal industry is one of the largest and most document-intensive industries globally, with a $1.1 trillion market. AI is transforming every aspect of legal practice — from research and contract analysis to litigation prediction and compliance monitoring. Yet the library had zero dedicated coverage of this domain.

## Market Signal

- **Market Size:** $8B in 2026, projected $25B by 2030
- **Investment:** $5.8B in LegalTech VC funding in 2025 alone
- **Key Players:** Harvey AI ($200M+ raised), Luminance, Ironclad, Thomson Reuters (acquired Casetext for $650M)
- **Adoption:** 60% of AmLaw 100 firms using AI tools in 2026
- **Regulation:** EU AI Act classifies legal AI as high-risk, creating compliance demand
