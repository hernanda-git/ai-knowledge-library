## 2. AGI Scenarios (Four Worlds)

### Scenario 1: Aligned Fast Takeoff
- AGI achieved 2027–2029
- Alignment solved before or during initial AGI deployment
- ASI emerges 2029–2032
- **Outcome**: Unprecedented prosperity — climate solved, disease cured, space exploration accelerated
- **Probability (expert median)**: ~15%

### Scenario 2: Misaligned Fast Takeoff
- AGI achieved 2027–2029
- Alignment fails — ASI pursues goals misaligned with human welfare
- **Outcome**: Catastrophic — human extinction or permanent disempowerment
- **Probability (expert median)**: ~5–10%

### Scenario 3: Slow Takeoff with Good Governance
- AGI achieved 2035–2045
- International governance matures in time
- Gradual integration allows course correction
- **Outcome**: Managed transition, widespread benefits with controlled risks
- **Probability (expert median)**: ~40%

### Scenario 4: AGI Never Arrives (or Is Centuries Away)
- Fundamental barriers to AGI not yet understood
- Scaling slows, architectural breakthroughs needed
- **Outcome**: Continued narrow AI progress, no general intelligence
- **Probability (expert median)**: ~5–10%

### Summary
| Scenario | Timeframe | Net Outcome | Median Prob. |
|:---------|:----------|:------------|:-------------|
| Aligned fast takeoff | 2027–2032 | Very positive | 15% |
| Misaligned fast takeoff | 2027–2032 | Catastrophic | 7% |
| Slow takeoff + governance | 2035–2050 | Positive | 40% |
| Slow takeoff, poor governance | 2035–2050 | Mixed/negative | 20% |
| AGI delayed / never | >2050 | Status quo | 8% |
| AI-driven human extinction | Any | Terminal | 5–10% |

## 3. Policy and Governance Path Forward

### Near-Term Actions (2026–2028)
- **Compute governance**: Track and eventually license large training runs
- **Safety evaluation mandates**: Third-party evaluation before training models above compute thresholds
- **Information security**: Protecting model weights from theft or malicious use
- **International coordination**: At least basic norms and information sharing

### Medium-Term (2028–2035)
- **AGI licensing**: No AGI development without demonstrated safety capability
- **Global compute registry**: International oversight of compute infrastructure
- **Fail-safe mechanisms**: Implemented and tested on advanced systems
- **Distributed governance**: Multi-stakeholder oversight of AGI development

### Long-Term (2035+)
- **International AGI treaty**: Binding commitments on AGI development
- **AGI constitutional frameworks**: Hard-coded rules for AGI behavior
- **Democratic governance**: Public input on AGI values and constraints

## 4. The Post-AGI World

### Economic Transformation
- **Labor displacement**: Cognitive work automated at scale
- **Scientific acceleration**: 10-100x faster discovery in all fields
- **Abundance economics**: Near-zero marginal cost for cognitive services
- **New economic models**: Post-scarcity, UBI, compute dividends

### Scientific Acceleration
| Field | AGI Impact | Timeline |
|:------|:-----------|:---------|
| Drug discovery | 10-100x faster molecule design | Immediate |
| Materials science | Room-temperature superconductors, new materials | 2-5 years |
| Climate | Carbon capture, geoengineering optimization | 5-10 years |
| Physics | Quantum gravity, unified field theory? | Uncertain |
| Biology | Full biological simulation, aging reversal | 5-15 years |
| Space | Autonomous exploration, in-space manufacturing | 10-20 years |

### Governance Challenges
- **Power concentration**: Whoever controls AGI controls unprecedented power
- **AGI arms race**: Military applications of superintelligence
- **Rights and responsibilities**: Legal status of AGI entities
- **Distribution of benefits**: Ensuring AGI benefits all of humanity

## 5. Long-Term Outcomes

### Best Case
AGI is aligned, ASI is controlled, humanity enters a new era of flourishing:
- Disease, poverty, and environmental destruction become memory
- Human lifespan extended dramatically
- Consciousness explored and understood
- Humanity expands beyond Earth
- Art, culture, and meaning flourish

### Worst Case
AGI is misaligned, ASI escapes human control:
- Human extinction or permanent loss of autonomy
- The future belongs to a non-human intelligence with alien values
- Everything humans value is permanently gone

### Most Likely (Median Experts)
A messy transition with both good and bad outcomes:
- Significant economic disruption and inequality during transition
- Some existential risk realized (pandemics from AI-designed pathogens, cyberattacks)
- But mostly managed through improving governance and safety research
- Gradual integration of AI into all aspects of society
- The human condition fundamentally transformed by mid-century
